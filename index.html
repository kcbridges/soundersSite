Welcome to my Sounders website!

I have created a few fun activities for you related to the Seattle Sounders FC. Please roam the site!


Sounders Trivia Challenge

In the Sounders trivia game, test your knowledge of the Seattle Sounders FC in the MLS era (2009-present). Some of the questions are tough! You may need to check out www.soundersfc.com to learn more about the players!

Soccer War Card Game

In this modified version of War, you are dealt a random hand of soccer trading cards, each player with attack and defense stats which face off against each other. You can hardly tell the difference between this game and a real soccer match!

Did you know?

The Sounders are one of only two teams to win three consecutive Lamar Hunt U.S. Open Cups!
